STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never disclose your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords to others.
2. Never click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify the sender before accepting any payment request.
4. Never accept “collect requests” unless you know the sender.
5. Never use unofficial banking apps for transactions.
6. Activate two-factor authentication for your accounts.
7. Always keep an eye on your bank statements for suspicious transactions.
8. Never use public Wi-Fi for transactions.

For reporting cybercrime in India:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
